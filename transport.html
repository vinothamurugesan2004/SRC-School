Safety Measures

SRC School prioritizes the safety of all students using our transportation services. Our safety measures include:

  1. Regular Maintenance : Buses undergo routine maintenance checks to ensure they are in top condition.
  2. Experienced Drivers : All drivers are licensed, trained, and undergo regular safety training.
  3. Safety Drills : Regular safety drills are conducted to prepare students and drivers for emergencies.
  4. GPS Tracking: All buses are equipped with GPS tracking for real-time monitoring.
  5. Onboard Cameras: Cameras are installed on all buses for added security.

Policies

Eligibility

Transportation is available to all students residing within a 20-mile radius of the school. Special accommodations can be made for students with disabilities.

Behavior Expectations

Students are expected to adhere to the following behavior guidelines while on the bus:

  1. 1. Remain seated and wear seatbelts at all times.
  2. 2. Follow the driver's instructions promptly.
  3. 3. No eating or drinking on the bus.
  4. 4. Keep noise to a minimum to avoid distracting the driver.
  5. 5. Respect fellow students and the bus property.
