About SRC School

Welcome to SRC School, where excellence in education meets holistic development. Established in 1995, SRC School has grown into a renowned institution dedicated to nurturing the intellectual, creative, and ethical growth of students. Our mission is to create a stimulating and inclusive learning environment that inspires students to achieve their full potential and become responsible global citizens.

Our Philosophy

At SRC School, we believe in fostering a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom. Our educational philosophy is rooted in the principles of academic rigor, character development, and community engagement. We aim to cultivate a passion for inquiry, critical thinking, and lifelong learning, while also emphasizing the importance of integrity, respect, and social responsibility.

Academic Excellence

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire students at every level. From foundational subjects to advanced courses, we offer a wide range of academic programs that cater to diverse interests and aspirations. Our dedicated faculty members are experts in their fields, committed to providing high-quality instruction and personalized support to each student. With a focus on developing strong analytical and problem-solving skills, our academic programs prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.

Co-Curricular Activities

SRC School recognizes the importance of a well-rounded education. Our extensive co-curricular program includes a variety of clubs, sports teams, and arts initiatives that encourage students to explore their interests and develop their talents. Whether it's through participating in a debate club, joining the soccer team, or performing in a school play, students have ample opportunities to grow outside the classroom and build valuable life skills.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities that provide an ideal setting for learning and growth. From advanced science laboratories and a well-stocked library to modern sports complexes and art studios, our resources are designed to support and enhance the educational experience. We are committed to integrating technology into our teaching practices, ensuring that students are well-equipped for the demands of the digital age.

Community and Social Responsibility

At SRC School, we believe in the power of community. Our students are encouraged to engage with and contribute to their local and global communities through various service-learning and outreach programs. These initiatives foster a sense of empathy, compassion, and civic responsibility, helping students to develop into conscientious and active citizens.

Inclusive Environment

Diversity and inclusion are core values at SRC School. We strive to create a welcoming environment where every student feels valued and supported. Our inclusive policies and practices ensure that all students, regardless of background, have access to the resources and opportunities they need to succeed. We celebrate the unique perspectives and experiences that each student brings to our community.

Parent and Guardian Partnership

We recognize that parents and guardians are essential partners in the educational journey. At SRC School, we maintain open lines of communication to keep families informed and engaged. Regular parent-teacher meetings, workshops, and seminars are held to ensure that parents are actively involved in their children's development. Together, we work towards the common goal of nurturing well-rounded, successful individuals.

Alumni Network

Our alumni network is a testament to the legacy of excellence at SRC School. Graduates of SRC have gone on to excel in various fields and make significant contributions to society. We are proud of their achievements and remain committed to supporting them as they continue to grow and succeed in their personal and professional lives.

Vision for the Future

Looking ahead, SRC School is dedicated to evolving and adapting to the changing educational landscape. We are committed to continuous improvement, innovation, and excellence in all that we do. Our vision is to provide the best possible education to our students, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to thrive in an ever-changing world.


Thank you for considering SRC School. We invite you to explore our website, visit our campus, and discover the vibrant, nurturing environment that makes SRC School a unique place for learning and growth.
